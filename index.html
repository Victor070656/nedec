About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Oilfield Services

Nedec Projects Limited is an indigenous oilfield service and supply company, proudly registered and incorporated in Nigeria as a Limited Liability Company since April 2005 (RC 620882).

With our head office in Lagos and operations base in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, we specialize in delivering high-quality services and products to the oil and gas industry, as well as civil construction.
