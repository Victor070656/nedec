About Nedec Projects Limited

Your trusted indigenous partner for oilfield services and civil construction since 2005

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2005

Nedec Projects Limited (Nedec) is a well-managed indigenous company focused exclusively on the oil and gas industry and civil construction. Established in April 2005, we operate with flexibility and adherence to multiple standards, codes, and policies to handle challenging situations effectively.

As a Nigerian company, we employ experienced professionals dedicated to superior job delivery. Our Port Harcourt base serves as the hub for all oilfield operations, ensuring efficient service across land, swamp, and offshore locations.

We are motivated by the Nigerian government's support for indigenous companies and strive to provide quality services that align with client needs.
