Our Policies

Committed to safety, sustainability, and responsible business practices

HSES Framework

Health, Safety & Environment

Our policies are guided by the CAHES (Community Affairs, Health, Environment, and Security) framework and Enhanced Safety Management (ESM) principles.

Policy 01

Leadership & Commitment

Our management is fully committed to providing a safe working environment for employees, subcontractors, communities, and stakeholders. We believe that leadership commitment is the foundation of effective HSES management.

Policy 02

Workforce Development

We prioritize manpower development, training, and motivation to foster excellence. Our people are our greatest asset, and we invest in their continuous professional growth and well-being.

Policy 03

Safety Policy

We ensure safe work procedures through our Community Affairs, Health, Environment, and Security (CAHES) Policy. Safety is integrated via Enhanced Safety Management (ESM) principles across all operations.

CAHES

Community Affairs, Health, Environment, Security

ESM

Community Affairs

We maintain strong relationships with host communities by communicating policies, listening to needs, and participating in social, educational, and cultural initiatives.

Health

We promote preventive healthcare, with policies on drugs, alcohol, and smoking. Retainer clinics include Data Medical Center and CUMI Medical Centre for immediate medical attention.

Environment

Committed to conservation with waste management, minimization, and eradication programs. We conduct our operations in an environmentally responsible manner.

Security

Top priority for personnel and assets, ensured by Nigerian Police Force, private security, and community guards. Our HSES function is led by a professional safety engineer.

Our HSES Commitment

Nedec Projects Limited is committed to conducting all business activities in a manner that ensures the health, safety, and security of our employees, contractors, communities, and the environment. We believe that zero incidents is achievable through proper planning, training, and execution.
