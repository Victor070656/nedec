# Home

## Welcome to Nedec Projects Limited

Nedec Projects Limited is an indigenous oilfield service and supply company, proudly registered and incorporated in Nigeria as a Limited Liability Company since April 2005 (RC 620882). With our head office in Lagos and operations base in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, we specialize in delivering high-quality services and products to the oil and gas industry, as well as civil construction.

Our commitment to excellence is driven by a team of experienced professionals who prioritize safety, innovation, and client satisfaction. We partner with global leaders in oilfield technology to provide reliable solutions that meet and exceed industry standards.
